Ultrasound Image Segmentation of Thyroid Nodule via Latent Semantic Feature Registration

summary

ASTN is a framework for thyroid nodule registration and segmentation.

By extracting latent semantic information from the atlas and target images and accomplish the registration of nodules, it optimizes the segmentation capacity of the model on different ultrasound venders.

  • Provably Accurate: shows high accuracy on different datasets of thyroid ultrasound images
  • Noval Algorithm: provides a well-founded method for weight calculation during label fusion of the warped atlas.
  • Generality: proves effective across different encoder-decoder architectures in segmentation models
